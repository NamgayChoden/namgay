Favorite destination

Bumthang

Bumthang in central Bhutan comprises four valleys - Choekhor, Tang, Chumey and Ura. These picturesque valleys are carved by ancient glaciers. It's a peaceful place with potatoes, rice and buckwheat fields. Hence, the name Bumthang translates directly to 'beautiful fields'.
